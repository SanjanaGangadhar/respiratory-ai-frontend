How It Works

Intelligent Respiratory
Disease Analysis

Our system leverages cutting-edge deep learning models to analyze both chest X-ray images and lung sound recordings, delivering accurate, explainable diagnoses in seconds.

Multimodal Input

Accepts both chest X-ray images and lung auscultation audio recordings for comprehensive dual-mode analysis.

  • Chest X-ray (DICOM/PNG/JPG)
  • Lung sound audio (WAV/MP3)
  • Drag & drop upload

AI-Powered Diagnosis

Deep learning CNN and spectrogram models classify diseases with high accuracy using trained medical datasets.

  • CNN image classification
  • Mel-spectrogram analysis
  • 96.8% accuracy rate

Real-time ESP32 Monitoring

Live lung sound capture using ESP32 microcontroller with digital stethoscope for continuous patient monitoring.

  • ESP32 + I2S microphone
  • WebSocket live streaming
  • Real-time waveforms

Grad-CAM heatmaps and LIME explanations provide transparent, interpretable reasoning for every diagnosis.

  • Grad-CAM heatmaps

Detectable Conditions

Respiratory Diseases
We Diagnose

Our AI system accurately identifies four major respiratory conditions using advanced deep learning on chest X-rays and lung audio patterns.

Chronic

Asthma

A chronic inflammatory condition causing airway narrowing, wheezing, and breathing difficulty. Detected via characteristic wheeze patterns in lung audio and hyperinflation on X-rays.

Key Symptoms

  • Wheezing & shortness of breath
  • Chest tightness
  • Coughing (especially at night)
  • Exercise-induced symptoms
Analyze Now
Progressive

COPD

Chronic Obstructive Pulmonary Disease causes persistent airflow limitation. Identified through crackle/wheeze audio patterns and emphysematous changes on chest X-rays.

Key Symptoms

  • Chronic breathlessness
  • Persistent mucus production
  • Frequent chronic cough
  • Reduced exercise tolerance
Analyze Now
Infectious

Tuberculosis

A bacterial infection (Mycobacterium tuberculosis) primarily affecting the lungs. Detected via cavitary lesions and infiltrates on X-rays, along with distinctive cough audio signatures.

Key Symptoms

  • Persistent cough (3+ weeks)
  • Blood in sputum (hemoptysis)
  • Fever & night sweats
  • Unexplained weight loss
Analyze Now
Acute

Pneumonia

An acute lung infection causing inflammation and fluid in air sacs. Identified via consolidation patterns on X-rays and crackle sounds in lung auscultation recordings.

Key Symptoms

  • High fever with chills
  • Productive cough with phlegm
  • Sharp chest pain on breathing
  • Rapid breathing & heart rate
